# Constellation Energy (constellation-energy)
Constellation Energy Corporation is the largest producer of carbon-free energy in the United States, operating a fleet of nuclear, wind, solar, natural gas, and hydroelectric generation facilities. The company serves approximately two million residential, public sector, and business customers, providing energy management tools and online account access. Constellation Energy does not currently offer a publicly documented developer API.
